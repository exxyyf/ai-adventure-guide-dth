## Description

Cultural heritage data shared with Europeana through our [network of aggregators](https://pro.europeana.eu/page/aggregators) is made publicly available through [Europeana.eu](https://www.europeana.eu/), giving educators, researchers, culture lovers and people everywhere the opportunity to explore Europe’s cultural heritage.
